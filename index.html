Find answers to common questions and make the most of your website experience.

What is MindMatters?

MindMatters is a website about mental health intended for the students. It contain hotlines for visitors to call in case of emergencies, information regarding mental health, and different features that would help in managing mental health problems.

Who can use MindMatters?

MindMatters is designed primarily for students, but anyone interested in mental health and well-being can benefit from its features. Whether you're dealing with stress, anxiety, or just looking for tools to maintain mental wellness, MindMatters is here to help.

What should I do if I need more help than MindMatters can provide?

While MindMatters offers a range of tools and resources, it is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you need more support, we recommend reaching out to a licensed therapist, counselor, or healthcare provider listed in the hotlines.
